About me

I am an entry-level Frontend Developer with a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, SASS, Bootstrap, vanilla JavaScript, TypeScript, UI/UX design, Figma and website building.
Prior to pursuing a career in IT, I spent 21 years working as a physiotherapist in a hospital setting. During this time, I developed a strong work ethic, excellent communication skills, and the ability to work under pressure while maintaining attention to detail as individual as well as in team.
My passion for website development grew while attending IT Academy in Sarajevo, where I gained valuable hands-on experience using industry-standard tools and techniques. I am eager to learn more and continue to improve my skills as I embark on this exciting new career path.
As a developer, I am committed to creating high-quality, user-friendly websites that provide the best possible experience for the end user. I am a responsible and reliable individual who takes pride in delivering work that exceeds expectations for contribution of your project.
